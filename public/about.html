About Page:

General: This site helps users to identify potential locations for them to start retail businesses within Prince George's County. We hope that this will be used as a tool for people to make millions!

Instructions: Simply modify the filters to suit your needs and we'll bring you a map of all building permits that fit your request.

Data: To gather data for this website, we had two primary data sources. The first data source came from PG County Census data. Additionally, we were able to webscrape data from retail business data to compliment the PG County census data. Our crack-team of data scientists then combined these datasets for your pleasure!
